About Us

What we do The Bit Buckets is a community-based STEM team that competes in robotics competitions and other technical challenges. Our middle-school team competes in First Lego League (FLL) and our high-school team competes in First Robotics Competition (FRC).

Our Mission Our mission is to motivate students with opportunities to solve realistic engineering problems. We enable team members to work on a team - and all aspects of running a team from fundraising to CAD, from programming to summer camps for middle and high school students.

Our Vision We envision a world where students are motivated to build productive relationships while responsibly applying knowledge through respect, leadership, and teamwork.
